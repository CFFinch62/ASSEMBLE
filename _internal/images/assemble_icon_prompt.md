# ASSEMBLE Icon - AI Image Generation Prompt

## For Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Adobe Firefly

---

### Concept Summary

ASSEMBLE is an Assembly language IDE. The icon depicts a **stone or metal wall** built entirely from Assembly language punctuation — `::` (labels), `=` (equates), `[` `]` (memory addressing brackets), `{` `}` (block braces) — arranged in staggered brick-like rows. The wall conveys structure, foundation, and machine-level solidity. A glowing capital **A** sits centered in the wall as if carved or illuminated from within. Color palette is grey-green oxidized metal — industrial, aged, precise.

---

### Primary Prompt

A square app icon with a very dark near-black green background (#080f09). The icon depicts a **solid wall** — a square or rectangular monolith — constructed entirely from glowing Assembly language punctuation characters: double colons `::`, equals signs `=` arranged in alternating horizontal rows like courses of bricks, staggered slightly each row like real brickwork. The left and right vertical edges of the wall are columns of alternating `[` `{` `[` `{` on the left and `]` `}` `]` `}` on the right, stacked vertically like structural pillars. The punctuation characters glow in muted grey-green and oxidized teal tones (#5a8060, #7ab87e, #4a7050). In the center of the wall is a large bold capital letter **A** glowing in warm white with a soft green radiant halo, as if a light source exists behind the wall at that point. The rows of `:: =` punctuation fade and part around the A to let it breathe. The overall feel is industrial, ancient machine, oxidized metal, structured and precise. Dark background, cinematic, high contrast, professional app icon, 1:1 square format, no text other than the letter A and punctuation symbols.

In HOPPER (COBOL) periods and hyphens form a punch card. For ASSEMBLE (Assembly), `:: =` characters and `[] {}` brackets should form a wall or monolith. Same glow quality, same dark background, same centered glowing letter."

### Tips for Best Results

- Upload the BLAZE icon as a style reference image — tell the AI "same glow style and dark background treatment as this icon, but wall instead of flame"
- If the wall reads as a flat rectangle, add: *"the wall should have visible depth and texture, the punctuation characters should appear to be raised or etched into metal plates"*
- If `::` and `=` aren't distinct enough: *"each :: and = should be individually legible, arranged like text characters, not abstract texture"*
- If the A gets lost: *"the letter A should be the single brightest element, glowing like a neon or backlit sign embedded in the wall"*
- Try adding *"like a monolith from 2001: A Space Odyssey but made of code"* for scale and gravitas
